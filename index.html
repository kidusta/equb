About E ቁ B

The Ethiopian Equb serves as a means of financial support and resource mobilization for individuals who may not have access to formal banking services. It promotes community cohesion, trust, and mutual support. However, it's important to note that participation in an Equb entails a level of commitment, trust, and understanding among the members, as defaulting on contributions can disrupt the system. It's worth mentioning that the specifics of Equb operations can vary depending on the group's agreed-upon rules, contribution amounts, and rotation frequency.

About Us
